What you can do

Not just booking — full calendar control from WhatsApp

Everything you need to manage your schedule — without leaving the app you're already in.

Book new meetings

Name a person, pick a time, set a duration — all in one message. Bot handles the invite and clash check.

"Book a 1-hour strategy session with the leadership team next Monday at 9am"

Reschedule on the fly

Change the time, date, or duration of any upcoming meeting without opening Outlook.

"Move my 2pm meeting with Tom to Thursday at 4pm instead"

Cancel meetings

Cancel any upcoming meeting and the bot notifies all attendees automatically.

"Cancel my Friday 10am team standup"

Check your schedule

Ask what's coming up today, tomorrow, or this week — a summary sent straight back to WhatsApp.

"What do I have on tomorrow afternoon?"

Clash detection

If a time is already taken, the bot flags it and suggests the nearest free slot automatically.

"Book Tuesday 10am with Lisa" → "That's taken — Tuesday 11am is free, shall I use that?"

Team meeting setup

Invite multiple people to one meeting in a single message. Bot checks all calendars and finds a common slot.

"Set up a 45-min all-hands with the operations team this week"
